# Code of Conduct

YIYUAN-ASSETS public-preview spaces are for bounded technical evaluation,
public-safe feedback, and respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussion relevant to the public-preview surface.
- Respect contribution, support, security, and licensing boundaries.
- Avoid sharing confidential, personal, customer, tenant, credential, or
  exploit information in public channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private vulnerabilities or exploit details.
- Uploading secrets, credentials, customer data, tenant data, or provider
  transcripts.
- Attempts to bypass protected core, commercial, security, or graph authority
  boundaries.
- Misrepresenting public preview as production support, commercial
  authorization, partnership, certification, or unrestricted open source.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, close, or remove public content that violates
this boundary. Maintainers may also limit participation when needed to protect
the project, contributors, users, or public-preview integrity.

For security-sensitive issues, use the private security reporting path described
in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not include sensitive details in public
issues.
